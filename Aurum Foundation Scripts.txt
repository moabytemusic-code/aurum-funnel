Using Your Dashboard
Step 5: Review your personal dashboard
Once logged in, you will land on your dashboard. This area shows:
• Your available balance in USDT
• Earnings from the last 7 days
• Affiliate overview
• Deposit packages
• Shortcuts for adding funds, withdrawing, and accessing the Aurum ecosystem
Step 6: Find your referral link
At the top of the dashboard, locate your personal referral link. You can use this to invite others to join Aurum.
Step 7: Review affiliate information
In the affiliate section, you can see:
• Your current partner level
• Progress toward the next level
• Affiliate leaderboard information
• Link to the Aurum partner Telegram channel
⸻
Funding Your Account
Step 8: Open the Finance section
From the left menu, click Finance. This section lets you:
• View your main balance
• See recent transactions
• Replenish your account
• Withdraw available funds
Step 9: Start a top-up
Under the Main Balance section, click Top Up
Step 10: Choose the asset
Select the cryptocurrency you want to deposit.
Example: USDT
Step 11: Select the network
Choose the network that supports your selected asset.
Example: TRON (TRC20) for USDT
Step 12: Continue to the wallet address
Click Continue to move to the next step.
Step 13: Send your deposit
On the replenishment screen:
• Copy the wallet address or scan the QR code
• Send the selected cryptocurrency to that address
• Make sure your deposit meets the minimum required amount
• Wait for the payment to be credited to your Main Balance
Important notes:
• The wallet address is only valid for the displayed time
• If your funds do not appear after about 30 minutes, contact support
• The guide states there is no commission for replenishing the account
⸻
Accessing the Aurum Ecosystem Subscription
Step 14: Open Aurum Ecosystem access
From the dashboard, click Access to Aurum Ecosystem in the Main Balance section.
Step 15: Check your available balance
Before subscribing, make sure your deposit meets the minimum funding requirement for the selected network.
Step 16: Confirm subscription
A confirmation pop-up will appear for the Aurum Ecosystem annual subscription.
• Click Confirm Purchase to proceed
• Click Cancel if you do not want to continue
Step 17: Verify activation
After purchase, your subscription status should show as Active.
You will also see:
• The next payment amount
• The next due date
• An option to cancel the subscription if needed
