Getting Started Guide

Step-by-step scripts and tutorials for navigating the Aurum Ecosystem.

1. Account Creation & Onboarding
  1. Step 1: Get Proton VPN (free).
  2. Step 2: Open the VPN.
  3. Step 3: (IMPORTANT: when signing up, for PHONE and COUNTRY fields choose "U.S. Minor Outlying Islands" if in the USA) then use your regular phone number.
  4. Step 4: Go to the Aurum login page. If you are new, click Sign Up to create an account.

Creating the Account

  • Enter your email address and phone number (Country code is Crypto Nation at the end of the list).
  • Set a strong password and enter the invite code from the partner who invited you.
  • Complete your profile: First/last name, nickname, DOB, and Country.
  • Accept Terms and click Sign Up.

Confirm Email

  • Check your inbox for the confirmation email from Aurum.
  • Copy the confirmation code and paste it into the confirmation field.
  • Click Confirm.
  • Dashboard Overview: Shows available balance in USDT, last 7 days earnings, affiliate overview, and shortcuts.
  • Referral Link: At the top of the dashboard, locate your personal referral link to invite others.
  • Affiliate Information: View your current partner level, progress toward next level, leaderboard, and link to the Aurum partner Telegram channel.
2. Funding & Transfers
  1. From the left menu, click Finance.
  2. Under the Main Balance section, click Top Up.
  3. Select the cryptocurrency (Example: USDT).
  4. Choose the network (Example: TRON (TRC20) for USDT).
  5. Click Continue to move to the wallet address.
  6. Copy the wallet address or scan the QR code.
  7. Send the cryptocurrency to that address (Make sure to meet the minimum required amount).
  8. Wait for payment to be credited (Usually within 30 minutes).

Note: There is no commission for replenishing the account. The wallet address is only valid for the displayed time.

In Coinbase:

  1. Click on Cash / USDC. (Or click the 3 dots and select Send)
  2. Click the Transfer button / Send.
  3. Click Exodus.
  4. Click Ethereum network.
  5. Change USD to USDC.
  6. Enter the amount and click Preview.
  7. Confirm details and click Send now.
  8. Check the new balance in your Exodus Wallet (This can take a minute).
  1. In Exodus, click on USDC.
  2. Click the swap icon (Left arrow above a right arrow).
  3. Swap from USDC to USDT. Enter the amount to the right of "USDC".
  4. Click the Swap icon.
  5. In your Aurum Account Dashboard, click on Top-up.
  6. Set Asset = USDT Tether, Network = BNB.
3. Ecosystem & Neyro Operations
  1. From the dashboard, click Access to Aurum Ecosystem in the Main Balance section.
  2. Ensure your deposit meets the minimum funding requirement.
  3. A confirmation pop-up will appear for the annual subscription. Click Confirm Purchase.
  4. Your subscription status should now show as Active. You will see next payment amount, due date, and cancellation options.

In the Aurum/Neyro web page:

  1. Click the “Connect wallet” button in top right.
  2. Click “Connect to WalletConnect”.
  3. Select the UniSwap Wallet (QR Code pops up).
  4. Click the "Top-up Agent" button, then "Connect to WalletConnect" again.
  5. Choose "Uniswap Wallet" and the QR Code window shows up.

In the Uniswap Wallet App:

  1. Click Swap. Enter amount, then change the top token to USDC.
  2. Click "Select token" and choose “Tether USD”.
  3. Click on the square QR Code Icon in the top right of the wallet app.
  4. Scan the QR code on Aurum.
  5. Click the Connect button in the Wallet.

Back in Aurum/Neyro:

  • A "Wallet Connect" window pops up. Click Close.

  2. Withdrawal will complete in about 1 minute.
  3. Your Aurum "Available profit" amount will go to zero.
  4. After several minutes, your Wallet balance for Tether USD will increase by the "Available profit" amount.
