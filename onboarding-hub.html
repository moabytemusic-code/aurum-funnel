Launch Initialization

Follow this 3-phase sequence to deploy your Aurum node and Neyro AI Agents.

1
Account Setup
2
Wallet Funding
3
Neyro Operations

1
VPN Configuration & Registration

Before connecting to the Aurum banking ecosystem, ensure your connection is properly routed.

  1. Download and open Proton VPN (Free).
  2. Connect your VPN to an approved region.
  3. Go to the Aurum sign up page.
CRITICAL FOR US USERS: When signing up, for both the PHONE and COUNTRY fields, you must select "U.S. Minor Outlying Islands". Then use your regular phone number. (Country code is Crypto Nation at the bottom of the list).

2
Complete Profile & Dashboard access

Finalize your account creation to unlock your master dashboard.

  • Enter your personal details (Name, Nickname, DOB).
  • Accept the Terms of Service and submit the form.
  • Check your email inbox, copy the confirmation code, and paste it into Aurum to verify.

Once logged in, locate your personal referral link at the top of the dashboard. This is your primary node link.

1
Route Funds: Coinbase to Exodus

Transfer capital from your primary exchange to your decentralized wallet.

  1. In Coinbase, select Cash / USDC and click Send/Transfer.
  2. Select the Exodus wallet as the destination.
  3. Select the Ethereum network and change currency to USDC.
  4. Preview the transfer and click Send now. (Wait ~1 minute to verify balance in Exodus).

2
Swap & Top-Up: Exodus to Aurum

Bridge your funds into the Aurum Ecosystem.

  1. In Exodus, click on USDC, then click the Swap icon.
  2. Swap your USDC to USDT.
  3. In your Aurum Dashboard, click on Finance > Top Up.
  4. Select Asset = USDT Tether and Network = BNB (or TRC20 based on preference).
  5. Scan the provided QR code or copy the address, and send the USDT from Exodus.

Your Aurum Main Balance will reflect the deposit within 30 minutes.

1
Activating Aurum Ecosystem

First, activate your annual node subscription.

  • Click Access to Aurum Ecosystem in your dashboard's Main Balance section.
  • Confirm the purchase (Ensure your balance meets the minimum).
  • Your status will update to Active.

2
Top-Up Neyro AI Agent

Connect your wallet to deploy capital into the AI trading agents.

  1. In the Aurum/Neyro page, click Connect wallet > WalletConnect.
  2. Select the Uniswap Wallet.
  3. Click Top-up Agent, and connect via WalletConnect again.
  4. In your Uniswap App: Click Swap -> Change top token to USDC -> Bottom token to Tether USD.
  5. Scan the QR code on Aurum using the Uniswap App.
  6. Click Connect in the Wallet.

3
Withdrawals & Harvesting

How to extract profits or capital from the Neyro ecosystem.

  • Withdraw Profit: Click the "Withdraw Profit" button. Your Aurum "Available profit" goes to zero, and your USDT Wallet balance will increase within 1 minute.
  • Withdraw Capital: Click "Close Agent". A 48-hour countdown begins. Once complete, your capital is released to your wallet.
